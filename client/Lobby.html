How to play

This game consists of 5 players, 4 drawers and 1 guesser.
The drawers are given a prompt in which they vote for their chosen theme.
They will then be given a scenario where they will need to draw, this is done collaboratively.
Each drawer will also have a side objective where they will need to draw a specific object/entity in said scenario.
It is the guessers objective to guess the scenario whilst also guessing the side objectives.
The guesser will lose time for wrong guesses and gain extra time for correct guesses until the timer runs out or they guess everything correctly.
